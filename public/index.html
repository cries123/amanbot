Features

Everything a serious trader needs

From Smart Money Concepts detection to macro event warnings — no tab-switching required.

📊

SMC Structure Scanner

Automated equal-high and equal-low wick detection on SPY, SPX, and QQQ using Yahoo Finance data.

  • 5m, 1h, and 4h timeframes
  • $0.05 wick tolerance (configurable)
  • Last 3 EQH + 3 EQL per scan
  • Discord embeds with touch times (EST)
⚡

On-Demand /flow Scan

Run a live EQH/EQL wick scan anytime. Pick your ticker and timeframe and get instant structure levels.

  • SPY, SPX, QQQ support
  • Real-time Yahoo OHLC data
  • Wick zone, touches, timestamps
🔗

TradingView Webhooks

Bridge your TradingView alerts into Discord. Optional — the built-in scanner works without TV.

  • EQH, EQL, FVG create & fill
  • Secret-protected endpoint
  • Rich embed formatting
📉

IV Extremes Monitor

Tracks realized volatility percentile across a 10-ticker watchlist. Alerts when options are historically cheap or expensive.

  • Scans at 9am, 12pm, 3pm ET
  • ≤10% = cheap premium signal
  • ≥90% = expensive premium signal
📅

Economic Calendar

Never get caught off-guard by CPI, FOMC, or NFP. Weekly digest plus 30-minute pre-event warnings.

  • Monday high-impact overview
  • @here warnings 30 min before
  • Estimate & previous values
🛠️

Trader Toolkit

Charts, news, and options math — all from slash commands in your server.

  • /chart — candlestick PNGs (1m–1D)
  • /news — live headlines + summaries
  • /breakeven — 6 options strategies
Commands

Slash commands at your fingertips

Type / in Discord to access every tool.

Command Access Description
/flow Everyone Live EQH/EQL wick scan — last 3 equal highs and lows on SPY, SPX, or QQQ
/chart Everyone Render a candlestick chart for any ticker (1m, 5m, 15m, 1h, 4h, 1D)
/news Everyone Latest headlines and summaries for any ticker
/breakeven Everyone Options breakeven, max reward, and max risk for 6 strategies
/smctest Admin Replay last session EQH/EQL scan for testing (all tickers supported)
/social Admin Post official Aman Trades social links
/ban /kick /mute Moderator Community moderation with role hierarchy checks
